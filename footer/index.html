VEFA Gallery is a contemporary art gallery bringing global artistic discourse to the South Bay. Our mission is to bring together artists from as wide a range of perspectives as possible around a topic that’s relevant today. We believe art is an excellent vehicle to educate and make positive contributions in the world.
