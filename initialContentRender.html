  The Grand Opening of VEFA Gallery. The Art of Surf is an homage to Southern California’s surf/skate culture. This group show explores the history of surfing while providing an insight to artworks today that embodies the ethos of what this unique catharsis of expression means to Southern California. This show will also explore the duality of how culture influences art and vice-versa.
