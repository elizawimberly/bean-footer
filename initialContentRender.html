The Art of Surf

The Inaugural Exhibition of VEFA Gallery

Homage to local surf/skate culture featuring
Debbie Daniels Jud Hogan HTJ Walter Erra Hubert Dennis Jarvis
David Krovblit RISK ROCK John De Temple Ben Thouard

VEFA Gallery is a contemporary art gallery bringing global artistic discourse to Southern California. Our mission is to bring together artists from as wide range of perspectives as possible around a topic that's relevant today. We believe art is an excellent vehicle to educate and make positive contributions in the world.
